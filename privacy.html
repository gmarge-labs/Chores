Privacy Policy

ChoreHeroes — Family Task Tracker

Last updated: April 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Gmarge Labs ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use ChoreHeroes ("the app") at choreheroes.app.

We take the privacy of children seriously. Please read this policy carefully before using the app.

1. Who We Are

ChoreHeroes is operated by Gmarge Labs. If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at heilleys@gmail.com.

2. Information We Collect

Account information (parents only)

  • Parent email address
  • Parent PIN (stored as a one-way cryptographic hash — we cannot recover it)
  • Family name and parent name

Children's information

  • Child's first name (provided by the parent)
  • Child's PIN (stored as a one-way cryptographic hash)
  • Task history, points, and rewards activity

Payment information

Payments are processed by Stripe. We do not store credit card numbers or payment details on our servers. Stripe's privacy policy applies to payment processing: stripe.com/privacy.

Technical information

  • Browser type and device type (for PWA functionality)
  • App usage data stored locally on your device

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the ChoreHeroes service
  • To process subscription payments via Stripe
  • To send task reminders and announcements via Home Assistant (Tier 2 subscribers only, using your provided webhook URL)
  • To respond to support requests
  • To improve the app

We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for advertising.

4. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

ChoreHeroes is designed for use by families. Children's accounts are created and managed entirely by a parent or guardian. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13.

  • All child accounts are created by the parent
  • Children's data (name, PIN, task activity) is stored under the parent's account
  • Parents can delete their account and all associated data at any time from within the app
  • We do not share children's data with third parties

If you believe we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child without parental consent, please contact us at heilleys@gmail.com and we will delete it promptly.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Firestore), which is hosted in the United States. Firebase enforces strict access controls — only authenticated account owners can access their family's data.

  • All connections to ChoreHeroes use HTTPS encryption
  • PINs are hashed using PBKDF2 with 100,000 iterations — we cannot read or recover them
  • Payment data is never stored on our servers

6. Data Sharing

We share data only with the following service providers, and only as necessary to operate the app:

  • Google Firebase — database and authentication
  • Stripe — payment processing
  • GitHub Pages — app hosting
  • Home Assistant / Nabu Casa — voice announcements (Tier 2 only, using your own webhook)

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any other third parties.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account from within the app, all associated data — including children's names, task history, and points — is permanently deleted from our systems within 30 days.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at heilleys@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Cookies and Local Storage

ChoreHeroes uses browser local storage to save your session and app data on your device. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics cookies.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
